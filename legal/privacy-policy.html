Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to ToolsHub ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser-based tools.

2. Browser-Only Processing (Important)

All file processing on ToolsHub happens entirely in your web browser. This means:

  • Your files never leave your device
  • No files are uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers
  • We do not store, access, or process your files on any backend infrastructure
  • All processing is done using Web Workers, Canvas API, and client-side JavaScript

This architecture ensures complete privacy and security for all your file operations.

3. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information:

  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (page views, tool usage counts) via privacy-friendly analytics
  • Local Storage: Preferences and settings stored only in your browser's local storage
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for site functionality (no tracking cookies)

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • File contents or metadata
  • Email addresses (unless voluntarily provided for support)
  • Payment information (all tools are free)

4. How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely for:

  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Understanding tool popularity and usage patterns
  • Maintaining site security and preventing abuse
  • Responding to user inquiries

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Vercel: Static hosting provider (no server-side processing)
  • Google Fonts: For typography (fonts are cached locally)
  • TailwindCSS CDN: For styling

None of these services receive or process your files.

6. GDPR Compliance

As a browser-only processing platform, we are inherently GDPR compliant:

  • Data minimization: We collect only essential information
  • Purpose limitation: Data is used only for stated purposes
  • Storage limitation: No persistent storage of user files
  • Security: Client-side processing eliminates server breach risks

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with the revised date.
