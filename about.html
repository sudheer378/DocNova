About BrowserTools

BrowserTools is a free, browser-based platform for processing PDF and image files. Our mission is to provide powerful tools that respect your privacy.

Why BrowserTools?

🔒 Privacy First

All processing happens in your browser. Files never leave your device.

⚡ Lightning Fast

No upload or download wait times. Instant client-side processing.

💰 Completely Free

No hidden fees, watermarks, or usage limits.

📱 Works Everywhere

Desktop, tablet, or mobile - works on any modern browser.

Our Technology

BrowserTools uses modern web technologies including Web Workers, OffscreenCanvas, and advanced JavaScript APIs to process files entirely in your browser. This approach ensures maximum privacy and performance.
