Frequently Asked Questions

Is this image compressor free? +
Yes, our image compressor is completely free to use with no limitations or watermarks.
Are my images uploaded to a server? +
No, all image processing happens entirely in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.
What image formats are supported? +
We support JPG, PNG, and WebP formats for both input and output. You can convert between these formats while compressing.
How much can I reduce image file size? +
Depending on the quality setting, you can reduce file size by 50-90% while maintaining good visual quality. Lower quality settings produce smaller files.
